About Mandara

Welcome to Mandara Yoga. I'm Mandara, and my mission is to help you discover the transformative power of yoga. Over the years, I've developed a unique approach that blends traditional yoga with modern insights. This combination aims to guide you toward a healthier and happier lifestyle. Join me on this journey of personal growth and well-being.

My story [ about the founder]

From a place of anger and disorganisation to calm and composure, my journey began with the power of yoga. Yoga chose me, becoming a transformative force in my life. Faced with health challenges like PCOS, I turned to yoga and a holistic lifestyle, leading to profound healing and self-discovery. My mission is to make yoga and a proper routine accessible and appealing to everyone, offering comprehensive support for a holistic lifestyle. Join me in creating a mindful, peaceful, and healthy world through the power of yoga and conscious living.
